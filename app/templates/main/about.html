About Imani Fellowship AGC

Imani Fellowship is a vibrant congregation of the Africa Gospel Church (AGC) located in Bomet, Kenya.

We are a Christ-centered community committed to worship, discipleship, fellowship, and serving our community. Our church is a place where people from all walks of life come together to grow in faith and share the love of God.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in Bomet and beyond through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To worship God, make disciples, serve our community, and share the Gospel to the ends of the earth.
